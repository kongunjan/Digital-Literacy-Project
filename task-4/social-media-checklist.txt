# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist






## Do's for Responsible Social Media Use
1. Think before posting and ensure that the content posted is respectful.
2. Maintain a professional and respectful online presence to ensure academic or career opportunities.
3. Ensure that the content posted is verified and accurate.
4. Use privacy settings to ensure that your personal information is not misused.
5. Engage in polite and respectful online communication.


## Don'ts for Responsible Social Media Use
1. Do not post content that is offensive, abusive, or harmful.
2. Do not disclose your personal information such as phone numbers, addresses, passwords, etc.
3. Do not engage in online rumors, fake news, and misinformation.
4. Do not post inappropriate content that may harm your reputation.
5. Do not engage in online arguments, bullying, and trolling.
