# Stay Safe Online Checklist




## Prevention Tips for College Students in India
1. Use strong and unique passwords for email accounts, social media, bank accounts, and college accounts.
2. Activate 2FA whenever possible to strengthen account security.
3. Never disclose your OTP, UPI PIN, CVV, ATM PIN, or passwords to anyone, even if they claim to represent the bank.
4. Refrain from scanning unknown QR codes or authorizing UPI collect requests unless you're making a payment.
5. Always be cautious while clicking on links sent through SMS, WhatsApp, email, or social media.
6. Refrain from downloading apps or APK files from unknown websites and only download them from the app store.
7. Ensure timely updates for your phone, laptop, browser, and antivirus to keep them safe from security threats.
8. While accessing the internet through public Wi-Fi, refrain from accessing banking apps or making transactions.
9. Regularly check account statements and UPI transactions to monitor any suspicious transactions.
10. Refrain from divulging personal details such as phone numbers, addresses, college ID cards, travel plans, and documents online.
